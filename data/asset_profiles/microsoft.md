# Microsoft Corporation (MSFT)

Microsoft is a multinational technology company founded in 1975, one of
the largest software and cloud-computing firms in the world. It trades on
the NASDAQ under the ticker MSFT and is a cornerstone of both enterprise
and consumer computing.

## What it is

Microsoft develops, licenses, and supports software, services, devices,
and cloud infrastructure. Its best-known products include the Windows
operating system, the Office and Microsoft 365 productivity suite, the
Azure cloud platform, the Xbox gaming business, and the LinkedIn
professional network. Azure and Microsoft 365 anchor a large,
subscription-based enterprise business.

## Why it exists

Microsoft's modern strategy centres on cloud and recurring software
revenue: moving customers from one-off licences to subscriptions and
consumption-based cloud services. Azure positions the company as one of
the two or three dominant providers of cloud infrastructure, while deep
enterprise relationships give it broad reach across business computing.

## Key facts

- **Sector**: Technology (Software & Cloud)
- **Exchange**: NASDAQ
- **Ticker**: MSFT
- **Founded**: 1975, Albuquerque, New Mexico (now Redmond, Washington)
- **Founders**: Bill Gates, Paul Allen
- **IPO**: March 1986

## Notable context

Microsoft's 1986 IPO created one of the largest concentrations of wealth
in technology. After a period of slower growth in the 2000s, a strategic
pivot to cloud under a new generation of leadership returned it to the
front rank of the industry. It is among the most heavily weighted
constituents of the S&P 500 and a major influence on the technology
sector.
